ABOUT -TECH MENTORS

"Tech Mentors"is online platform specially developed for srilankan students which is administration under the Open University of Sri Lanka.
The main stimulate for develop this "Tech Mentors" online program is the current situation due to COVID-19 pandemic.
This online program offers a unique platform for Sri Lankan students.
Here you can achieve your study goals while enjoying the flexibility offered by studying online.
We are giving students a great support team ,online learning advisors, assessment assistance.
We build our learning environment in the way that makes it easy for student to get in touch with each other.
Studying with us is all about you ,your future, your growth and your opportunities. welcome to "Tech Mentors" online.
